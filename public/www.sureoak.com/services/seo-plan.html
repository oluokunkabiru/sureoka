What is the SEO Game Plan?

The Game Plan is Sure Oak’s proprietary, holistic approach to building a solid foundation for your SEO, your blueprint for unlimited growth. Through this proven process we can increase your website’s search rankings, promote more organic leads, drive sales, and amplify profitability for your business. We do this by focusing on the ranking factors that will make the most impact — identifying all roadblocks, opportunities, and strategies to grow your organic success long-term.

Content Strategy

Our analytics and keyword research drive the right content strategy for your business. Authentic content applied following our proven methodology forms a powerful path to generating sustainable growth.

Page Content Analysis

We pick apart your website to see it the way Google does. By providing a detailed analysis of what is working and what needs work, we create a roadmap of how your pages need to look from a content perspective.

Keyword Research

Our multi-layered keyword research pinpoints exactly which keywords to incorporate to increase your ranking through our sophisticated mapping tools.

Content Development Strategy

A highly intentional content development strategy will do wonders for your traffic. We help you determine what needs to happen and when to get you where you want to be.

On-Site SEO

A deep dive into the inner workings of your site to uncover the issues that are holding you back and specific opportunities to rank higher.

Technical SEO

Our technical SEO audits spot what others miss. Unlike a simple automated tool, we personally check for technical issues, ensuring no stone is left unturned.

On-Page Optimization

From metas to H1 tags, we make sure your page is optimized, so search engines can crawl across your content and understand it. The result? Your customers can easily find you.

Analytics & Search Console

Understand your organic traffic, the behavior of your visitors, and the nature of your conversions to see where your site stands and what needs work.

Site Architecture & UX

Get an honest look at how your site’s architecture and navigation are impacting user experience and how these facets can be improved upon to drive growth.

Off-Site SEO

The strongest determinants of SEO success are found in off-site strategies. Learn how to harness these powerful techniques that have a huge impact on the bottom line.

Backlink Analysis

Who is linking to your site? A comprehensive backlink analysis will tell us exactly where your domain stands and what steps to take to get you ranking higher.

Link Building Strategy

Link building is more than a science, it’s an art built on relationships. And it’s a scientific art we have mastered. We are able to get links no one else can through the connections we’ve established over the years. These real connections are potent drivers of Google’s #1 ranking factor.

Competitor Analysis

How are your competitors winning? What strategies are they using to get ahead? We analyze the behavior of your competition, uncovering insights into what approaches will work for your business.
