What is Local SEO?

Local SEO is the practice of optimizing your online presence to get more traffic, customers, and brand awareness from local search results.

In the modern age, Google is the new Yellow Pages. When potential customers are searching for what you offer, Local SEO puts you at the top of the list. This is huge for local businesses.

  • 90% of consumers use search engines to find local businesses.
  • 92% of searchers pick local businesses on the first page of Google.
  • 76% of people who conduct a local search on their phone visit a business within 24 hours.

Bottom line? Local search is exploding, and getting in front of these searchers (with Local SEO) is a tremendous opportunity to attract more business.

Local SEO: Frequently Asked Questions

What is a local search?

When someone uses a search engine to find a business or service nearby, they are performing a local search. Typically resulting in a “local pack” of businesses in the same area, local search is the natural progression of traditional advertising (Yellow Pages, newspaper ads, etc.)

How can my business benefit from Local SEO strategies?

Local SEO is so effective because it targets users in your area who are ready to buy. By appearing at the top of local search results, you reach potential customers exactly when they’re interested in your product or service. As a marketing strategy, this results in highly-qualified leads and, in turn, more customers.

How does Local SEO work?

Local SEO starts by choosing relevant keywords that you would like to rank for. To help your business rank higher for those keywords, we perform a series of tasks, including:

  • content optimizations;
  • website optimizations;
  • local profile optimizations;
  • review management;
  • more….

By improving your relevance, authority and experience, we make it easier for search engines to trust your business website. This results in higher rankings and more customers for your business!

How much does Local SEO cost?

The price of Local SEO depends on two things: your needs and your competitors.

Businesses with tough competitors and no past SEO experience will need a lot of attention in order to rank at the top. On the other side, businesses with weak competitors and an optimized website will call for less effort. This is why we approach each client’s project on a case-by-case basis, only providing the tactics and services that are necessary.

If you’d like to get a better idea of where your SEO stands vs. where it needs to be, schedule a free consultation with us. Once we assess your needs and your position in the market, we’ll provide you with an estimate.

Why should I hire someone else to handle my SEO?

Google uses over 200 variables to determine search rankings. While you can perform local SEO on your own, it will take several months to learn about all of the elements of a successful SEO strategy. Not only will your results be delayed, but you’ll end up using a lot of time that could be spent on other aspects of your business.

SEO is a skill that requires time, experience, and dedication. Most small business owners don’t have the time to learn local SEO, and that’s why they choose a trusted partner to solve these problems efficiently.
