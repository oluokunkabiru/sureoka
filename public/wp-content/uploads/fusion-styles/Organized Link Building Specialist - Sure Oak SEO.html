Organized Link Building Specialist

  • Remote Job
  • Part-time / Full-time

Our SEO agency is looking for an awesome link-building specialist that is comfortable implementing various link-building strategies for our clients. Our company culture is fun, supportive, forward-thinking, and open-minded. We are looking for someone who is all-around awesome, smart, focused, self-directed, and efficient. This is a unique opportunity for a long-term position in a growing startup.

Job Description:

Our link-building specialist will be an important member of our off-site team. They will be responsible for building links for our various clients and assist in implementing strategies.

The ideal candidate will be comfortable engaging with website owners and bloggers to establish rapport with these contacts and ultimately encourage them to share and backlink to our content. This requires emailing contacts from our database, sharing valuable content in these emails (infographics and articles), tracking any responses, and negotiating with contacts for backlinks.

Required Skills:

  • Strong organizational skills
  • Persistent
  • Intuitive and communicative
  • Ability to meet deadlines

Desired Skills:

  • Understanding white hat SEO link building techniques
  • Experience working on link building campaigns

Why Join Us:

  • Work from home
  • Flexible hours (needs to be available during normal business hours)
  • Be part of a fun, awesome, and positive team
  • Improve your skills, learn more SEO, and advance your career
  • Opportunities for promotion
